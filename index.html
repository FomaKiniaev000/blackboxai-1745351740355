Welcome to Budget-OS

Budget-OS is a web-based cloud PC platform designed to provide you with a powerful, accessible, and cost-effective computing environment from anywhere in the world.

Why Budget-OS?

  • Access your personal cloud PC from any device with a modern browser.
  • Low cost or free access to computing resources.
  • Secure and private environment with multi-level access control.
  • Easy to use interface with powerful workspace features.
  • Open source and community-driven development.
